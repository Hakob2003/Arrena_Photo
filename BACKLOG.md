# Project Backlog & Ideas

## Saved Ideas (2026-06-24)
These ideas were proposed and saved for later implementation.

1. 🎨 **Inpainting & Canvas Editor**
   After generating an image, give the user a brush to paint over a part of the image and click "Redraw" (Inpainting). Or just a convenient crop/correction directly in the browser.

2. 🧑‍🦱 **LoRA Training Dashboard (Train Your Own Face)**
   A section where a user uploads 10 selfies, we train a LoRA model, and then they can select themselves as a "Model" in all platform templates! (Must-have for AI photo services).

3. ✨ **Magic Prompt (AI Prompt Enhancer)**
   A magic wand button in the template editor. The user writes "Cat in space", clicks the button, and an LLM expands it to "Cinematic lighting, 8k resolution, cat wearing astronaut suit...".

4. 🔍 **Post-processing (Upscale and Face-Fix)**
   Add buttons to generated photos: "Make 4k (Upscale)" and "Fix Face (Face Restoration)". These are separate micro-generations.
